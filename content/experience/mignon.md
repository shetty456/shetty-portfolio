# Mignon

**Role**: Co-founder
**Duration**: 2022 — 2023

## Context
Mignon is a B2B SaaS platform for salon booking. Sunil co-founded the company and led product discovery, experimentation, and early GTM execution.

## Key Accomplishments

- Led pivot from feature-driven development to demand-first GTM

- Redesigned value proposition around salon owner ROI
- Resulted in measurably higher-quality inbound leads and improved demo conversion

### User Research
- Conducted primary user research with 20+ salons
- Uncovered key discovery and booking pain points that informed product direction

### Growth Experiments
- Built push-notification and re-engagement experiments
- Tested PLG loops and repeat usage hypotheses

### End-to-End Ownership
- Owned product discovery, experimentation, and early GTM execution
