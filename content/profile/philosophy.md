# Sunil's Product Philosophy

## 1. Experimentation Over Opinion
Every product decision should be a hypothesis. Sunil designs experiments before writing PRDs, ensuring that features are validated through real user behavior rather than internal assumptions. He has run 90+ A/B tests across retention, onboarding, monetization, and engagement surfaces.

## 2. Retention Before Acquisition
Growth without retention is a leaky bucket. Sunil's first question for any product is: "Do our existing users come back?" He builds retention loops before scaling acquisition, resulting in measurable improvements like 15% → 28% D30 retention.

## 3. Systems Thinking
Great products are interconnected systems. Sunil maps the full user journey — acquisition, activation, retention, revenue, referral — before optimizing any single surface. He looks for second-order effects and unintended consequences of feature changes.

## 4. User Empathy + Data Together
Neither pure data nor pure intuition is sufficient. Sunil pairs quantitative signals (DAU, retention curves, funnel drop-offs) with qualitative research (user interviews, session recordings, support ticket analysis) to build a complete picture of user needs.
